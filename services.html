Our 8-Step Deep Cleaning Process

1

Pre-Inspection

Our technician walks through the area with you, noting any stains or areas of concern and setting expectations.

2

Home Protection

We protect hardwood floors, corners, and delicate surfaces with guards and cloths before any work begins.

3

Furniture Moving

We move light furniture free of charge. Heavy or valuable items can be handled with care for a small extra fee.

4

Pre-Spray

We apply a gentle but powerful pre-conditioner to break down embedded soil and loosen grime.

5

Pre-Spot Treatment

We target difficult stains with custom solutions based on stain type and surface material.

6

Soil Extraction & Rinse

High-powered, pH-balanced steam extraction eliminates dirt without overwetting or residue.

7

Post-Spot Treatment

If any spots remain, we give them additional attention using specialty techniques and products.

8

Final Walkthrough

We walk through the area with you again to ensure your complete satisfaction and answer any questions.
